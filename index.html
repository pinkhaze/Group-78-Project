Overiew of Project

 

Metropolis Property Management (MPC) is a newly founded landlord in the residential neighborhood of Maplewood Haven. A year ago, MPC acquired its first property, SkyTower Heights, which is made up of 500 rental units. At the moment, SkyTower Heights has 698 tenants and generated $7.3 million in rental revenue last year.

On average, SkyTower Heights receives 400 maintenance requests per month, which are serviced by a team of 15 maintenance workers. SkyTower Heights partners with a total of 6 utility providers to supply electricity, gas, water, and waste disposal services. Tenants can choose from 3 cable and 2 high-speed internet providers.

At the moment, SkyTower Heights does not have an efficient database system. A data-driven website will record all information necessary to efficiently manage SkyTower Heights. Entities that will be tracked include units, rental agreements, maintenance requests, tenants, maintenance workers, request assignments, and utility providers. Using this data-driven website will allow management to efficiently track vital business information and provide a mechanism for management to analyze data collected.
